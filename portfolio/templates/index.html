ABOUT ME

I am Mirko Zeppieri, a Photo Retoucher and Digital Designer, born and raised in Rome, currently based in Amsterdam, the Netherlands. I attended the Instituto Europeo di Design (IED) in Rome, where I earned my Bachelor's Degree in Photography and Retouching. I have a strong background in photography, which helps me in putting together the images that I want to create, as I work extensively in the studio on commercial photography, mainly for advertising and e-commerce, as well as for stock photo websites as an author and contributor. I am open to any type of business proposal or partnership; if you want to contact me, I will be happy to answer.
